The TechXtract Manifesto

TechXtract: Neon Pulse is more than just a society; it's the heartbeat of innovation within the Computer Science & Technology department at MAIT. Established in 2023, we are dedicated to extracting and amplifying the innate talent of every student.

Our Mission - Illuminate and Accelerate

To provide a dynamic, hands-on environment that bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world technology. We foster a culture of constant learning, collaboration, and ethical development, ensuring our members are future-ready tech leaders.

Our Vision - The Future is Here

To be recognized as the most impactful student-run tech body, driving the adoption of cutting-edge technologies and producing a pipeline of skilled, industry-ready professionals who redefine the technological landscape.

Key Focus Areas

  • Workshops & Training: Deep-dive sessions on AI/ML, Cloud Computing, DevOps, and Full-Stack Development.
  • Competitions: Organizing hackathons and coding challenges to promote competitive skill development.
  • Mentorship: Connecting students with alumni and industry experts for career guidance and project mentorship.
